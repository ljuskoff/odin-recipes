Milanesa Napolitana

Description

Another Buenos Aires' must-try.

Think of the most tender beef or chicken, perfectly breaded and deep fried, only for then be topped with tomato pizza sauce and mozzarella cheese. This dish goes hand in hand with salad, mashed potatoes or fries of any kind! Prepare yourself for a delight!

Ingredients

  • beef meat or chicken breast, 7 mm slices - 1 Kg
  • Eggs - 4 units
  • Chopped garlic - 1 spoonful
  • Chopped parsley - 1 spoonful
  • Mustard - 1 spoonful
  • Milk - 1 coffee cup
  • Shredded bread - Required quantity
  • Frying oil - Required quantity
  • Flour - 1 cup
  • Pizza tomato sauce - 1 cup
  • Mozzarella cheese - 500 gr
  • Slices of round tomatoes - Required quantity
  • Oregano - 1 spoonful

Step by step

  1. Clean and extract all the fat and tissues from the outer part of the meat.
  2. Add salt and pepper to taste and hammer under plastic foil the pieces to break the superficial structures.
  3. Slightly pass both sides of the pieces by flour.
  4. Prepare the huevada: Wisk the eggs with the milk, add salt and pepper, garlic, parsley and mustard. Mix all the ingredients and let the meat pieces rest in the preparation for at least 3 hours at the fridge. Even, if possible, overnight.
  5. Afterwards, pass the meat pieces by shredded bread, also condimented and firmly pressing against so the bread can stick well. Kudos! You now have "Milanesas".
  6. Take the milanesas to the fridge and let them rest for a while.
  7. Fry the milanesas in a good amount of oil for two minutes.
  8. Let the milanesas rest to dry over tissue paper. Add the pizza tomato sauce and cheese on top. Optional: a slice of ham, under the cheese. Also add the slices of tomato.
  9. Take your milanesas to oven at maximum until melting the cheese.
  10. Finish with oregano and chopped garlic over the tomato slices.
  11. Serve with salad, fries or mashed potatoes.
