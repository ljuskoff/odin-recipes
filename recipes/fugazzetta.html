Fugazzetta

Description

This is a Buenos Aires' classic.

If you are in for a crusty dough, fine caramelised onions and a filling exploding of mozzarella cheese and ham, do not wait any longer and get ready for a treat!

Ingredients

  • 0000 Flour - 1/2 Kg
  • Water - 300 cc
  • All purpose oil - 4 spoons
  • Yeast - 20 gr
  • Mozzarella - 300 gr
  • Cuartirolo cheese - 200 gr
  • Ham - Required quantity
  • Onions - 2 units
  • Oregano - Required quantity
  • Olive oil - Required quantity
  • Diced provolone - Required quantity

Step by step

  1. Set the flour and the salt over the table in a crown fashion. Pour the liquids and the yeast at the center and knead until you get a smooth dough. Make two pieces of 500 gr and 300 gr. Let them rest.
  2. Finely slice the onions, set them in a bowl, add salt and iced water.
  3. Once raised, degasificate the pieces of dough, spread the 500 gr one and cover a pizza dish previously oiled.
  4. Grate the mozzarella and slice the Cuartirolo cheese.
  5. Cover the spreaded dough with ham, top it with the cheeses and a pinch of dried oregano. Cover with the 300 gr dough and press the edges firmly.
  6. Rinse the onions well, and if necessary, blot them dry with a dishtowel. Top with the onions the surface of the preparation.
  7. Cook in medium/high oven for about 30 minutes. When 10 minutes left, add the provolone diced cheese on top. Set the oven to maximum and grill.
